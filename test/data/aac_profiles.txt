────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────


 1 USAC (AOT 42) is the most advanced AAC profile with dual coding modes
 2 Three modes: SWITCHED (adaptive), FD (music), TD (speech)
 3 Enhanced SBR provides better high-frequency reconstruction than legacy SBR
 4 Frame lengths: 768 or 1024 samples (vs 1024 for AAC-LC)
 5 Advanced features: Complex prediction, PVC, harmonic SBR, inter-TES
 6 Use cases: Universal - works for speech, music, and mixed content
 7 Bitrate range: 24 kbps (mono speech) to 192+ kbps (stereo high quality)
 8 eSBR ratios: 8:3, 2:1, or 4:1 upsampling for bandwidth extension

                                            Quick Start Recommendations

                                                     For Music:


.\xaacenc.exe -ifile:test_2MB.wav -ofile:test2_aot42.aac -aot:42 -usac:1 -br:128000 -ccfl_idx:3 -esbr:1 -harmonic_sbr:1
.\xaacdec.exe -ifile:test2_aot42.aac -imeta:test2_aot42.txt -ofile:output.pcm -mp4:1
                                                                                5

.\xaacenc.exe -ifile:test_5MB.wav -ofile:test5_aot42_0.aac -aot:42 -usac:0 -br:24000 -ccfl_idx:3 -esbr:1 -harmonic_sbr:1
.\xaacdec.exe -ifile:test5_aot42_0.aac -imeta:test5_aot42_0.txt -ofile:output.pcm -mp4:1


                                                    For Speech:

 -aot:42 -usac:2 -br:32000 -ccfl_idx:0


                                        For Mixed Content (Podcasts, etc.):

 -aot:42 -usac:0 -br:64000 -ccfl_idx:1 -tns:1 -nf:1


                                                For Maximum Quality:

 -aot:42 -usac:1 -br:192000 -ccfl_idx:3 -esbr:1 -esbr_hq:1 -harmonic_sbr:1 -pvc_enc:1 -inter_tes_enc:1 -cmpx_pred:1
